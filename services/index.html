Search Engine Optimization Service

My expertise is on-page and site-wide technical SEO. I know how to approach SEO effectively; what is important and what’s not. I’m well familiar with the original “Google Search Central Documentation”.

I have created this website myself, including the layout, without the help of CMS. As an SEO, understanding web technologies allows me to well understand developers, thus effectively collaborating with them.

Here is the coverage of aspects I consider, doing Search Engine Optimization:

  • General analysis and issues.
    • I will identify competing websites as well as analyze them to get a realistic view on their strength and abilities.
      • Among other things, includes layout, UX and trust signals suggestions based on the observation of competing sites.
    • I will setup Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics for tracking website events.
    • I will fix internal and external broken (404) links.
  • Technical optimization. I will ensure proper robot’s behavior on the site:
    • what’s not to crawl; what’s not to index;
    • specifically, that bots don’t waste crawling time on junk and anything they shouldn’t crawl (pages that not suppose to be in search, “infinite spaces” and duplicate pages), which ensures general high quality content; if crawl budget is a concern - the absence of the general quality might prevent important pages from being crawled and indexed;
    • the look of pages’ snippets in SERPs;
    • giving Google all the hints to pick the right canonical and correctly associate duplicates with their respective canonical pages.
  • Content strategy and keyword/topic research.
    • I will do comprehensive topic research (among other types, outlining “monetization potential” topics).
    • I will provide suggested site structure, based on main keywords & taking into account clients’ preferences.
    • I have an effective method of researching other topics that are of most need to users, beyond topics that are part of the main site structure.
  • Content creation/maintenance workflow.
    • I’m familiar with web technologies as well as Git and GitHub, so I can work closely with developers and make sure it is easy for them to see and implement any small changes to the content.
    • I will guide the writer on how to write high quality content that has ranking potential, instead of further building on something that will not rank high anyway;
    • I will make sure that all the necessary aspects has been taken into account while creating new pages:
      • page is targeting all the search terms it should, without doing unnatural keywords stuffing;
      • proper title and description;
      • meaningful internal links from and into the page;
      • applicability for relevant Rich Results;
      • internationalization if applicable;
      • accessibility to user with disabilities, specifically optimizing for screen readers, which also helps search engines understand the site; this include making sure all images have relevant text in their “alt” attributes;
      • updated sitemap;
      • kickstarting Natural Link’s Acquisition with help of external or/and internal links, if necessarily (think as LinkBuilding).
  • Internal linking management (controlling how traffic is being distributed within the site).
    • I will make sure that pages are linked meaningfully and traffic is distributed to pages that need it, without taking it away from other pages that rely on it.
  • Page Experience.
    • Based on Real Users Data on CWV as well as data from Lighthouse (lab data tool), I will help developers to identify issues with highest impact on how users perceive loading of key types of pages.
  • LinkBuilding.
    • I will find the best websites to target your site’s content towards and potentially reaching out to.
    • I will periodically reach out to these websites and let them know about the new great content on the your website.
